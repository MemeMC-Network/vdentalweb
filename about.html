About Velic Dental Arts Studio

Excellence in Dental Laboratory Services

Premier Dental Laboratory Excellence

Velic Dental Arts Studio LLC is a premier dental laboratory located in Wethersfield, Connecticut, dedicated to creating exceptional custom dental prosthetics that blend artistry with advanced technology.

Our Mission

We serve as your trusted behind-the-scenes partner, providing dentists with the finest quality crowns, bridges, veneers, and implants. Our commitment to excellence and precision ensures that every restoration we create meets the highest standards of craftsmanship and aesthetics.

Who We Serve

As a specialized dental laboratory, we don't provide direct patient care. Instead, we empower dental professionals with the tools they need to deliver beautiful, lasting smiles to their patients. Our clients are distinguished dental practices that demand the highest level of quality and service.

Our Approach

Every restoration that leaves our studio represents a perfect marriage of art and science. We combine traditional craftsmanship techniques with cutting-edge digital technology to create restorations that are not only functional but also aesthetically stunning.

Location & Facilities

Based in Wethersfield, Connecticut, our state-of-the-art laboratory is equipped with the latest in digital dentistry technology, allowing us to provide precise, efficient, and predictable results for every case we handle.
