Meet Our Founder

Master Dental Technician & Visionary Leader

Ermin Velic, MDT

Master Dental Technician & Founder

A Passion for Excellence

Ermin Velic is an award-winning Master Dental Technician whose passion for excellence and artistry has established Velic Dental Arts Studio LLC as a leader in the dental laboratory industry. His dedication to combining traditional craftsmanship with modern digital technology has earned him recognition as one of the field's most respected experts.

Background & Expertise

With a distinguished background in ceramics and restorative dentistry, Ermin founded Velic Dental Arts Studio to provide dental professionals with premium, artistically crafted restorations that exceed expectations. His meticulous attention to detail, deep understanding of dental anatomy, and commitment to continuous innovation ensure that every piece leaving the studio represents the highest standards of quality and aesthetics.

Philosophy & Vision

Ermin's philosophy centers on the belief that every restoration is a work of art. He approaches each case with the same level of dedication and craftsmanship, whether it's a single crown or a complex full-mouth reconstruction. His vision for Velic Dental Arts Studio is to be the preferred laboratory partner for discerning dental professionals who demand nothing but the best for their patients.

Innovation & Technology

As an early adopter of digital dentistry technologies, Ermin has seamlessly integrated cutting-edge CAD/CAM systems and 3D printing into the studio's workflow while maintaining the artistry and precision that can only come from master-level craftsmanship. This unique combination of traditional skill and modern technology sets Velic Dental Arts Studio apart in the industry.
