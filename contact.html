Frequently Asked Questions

What is a dental laboratory?

A dental laboratory creates custom dental prosthetics (crowns, bridges, veneers, etc.) for dentists. We work behind the scenes to craft the restorations that dentists place in their patients' mouths.

Do you see patients directly?

No. We are a dental laboratory that works exclusively with licensed dental professionals. If you need dental care, please contact your dentist.

How do I become a partner practice?

Contact us using the form above or call us directly. We'll discuss your practice's needs and explain our process for onboarding new partners.

What is your turnaround time?

Turnaround times vary depending on the type of restoration and case complexity. Contact us to discuss specific timelines for your cases.

Do you accept digital scans?

Yes! We work with digital scans from all major intraoral scanning systems, as well as traditional impressions.

What areas do you serve?

While we're based in Wethersfield, CT, we serve dental practices throughout Connecticut and beyond through our shipping services.
