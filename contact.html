Frequently Asked Questions

What is a dental laboratory?

A dental laboratory creates custom dental prosthetics (crowns, bridges, veneers, etc.) for dentists. We work behind the scenes to craft the restorations that dentists place in their patients' mouths.

Do you see patients directly?

No. We are a dental laboratory that works exclusively with licensed dental professionals. If you need dental care, please contact your dentist.

How do I become a partner practice?

Contact us using the form above or call us directly. We'll discuss your practice's needs and explain our process for onboarding new partners.

What is your turnaround time?

Standard turnaround is 10-14 business days. Rush cases can be accommodated based on availability. Contact us to discuss your specific timeline needs.

What materials do you work with?

We work with premium materials including IPS e.max, Bruxzir, Prettau zirconia, Noritake porcelain, and high-noble gold alloys. All materials are FDA-approved and biocompatible.

Do you accept digital impressions?

Yes! We accept digital impressions from all major intraoral scanner systems including iTero, CEREC, 3Shape TRIOS, and others. We can also work with traditional impressions.

What areas do you serve?

While based in Wethersfield, Connecticut, we serve dental practices throughout the state and neighboring regions. We offer convenient shipping options for all locations.

Do you offer shade matching services?

Absolutely. We provide comprehensive shade matching using digital spectrophotometry and traditional shade guides. We can also visit your office for complex cases.

What if the restoration doesn't fit?

We stand behind our work with a fit guarantee. In the rare event of a fit issue, we'll remake the restoration at no charge. Our precision manufacturing ensures first-time fit accuracy.
